Time Till

Time Till is the modern timer.

Beautiful Timekeeping

Timekeeping has never been easier without this flat, minimalistic design.

Multiple Timers

Have as many timers as you want. Time Till saves them all.

Count Up and Down

Time Till shows times till and since dates, giving you full awareness of elapsed periods.
These timers show the lengths of time between now and the next and previous Christmases.

Date Validation

Time Till validates your dates for you, so you don't have to.

Time Till supports counting up from a time in the past, but just in case, it warns you.
